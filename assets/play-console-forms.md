## Notification listener permission declaration

> The app's core function is to capture notification content from user-selected apps and forward it to a user-configured webhook URL. The Notification Listener permission is required to read notification title and body text. Without it, the app cannot function. No notification data is sent to the developer — it is forwarded exclusively to the endpoint the user provides.
